StreamDeckAudioDownloader

Convert your Stream Deck audio files to a downloadable format.

Choose between two methods

SDA2Wav

Download audio files from your Stream Deck and convert them to downloadable .wav files.


This code was adapted from RaineAllDay's SDA2Wav project. Without her work, the following page wouldn't exist.

Catalog Scraper

Download audio files from your Stream Deck.


This project was made by me. I realised catalog.json contains preview urls, which contains the whole MP3 files.

Notice:

You will need the Stream Deck software installed on your computer, and to have downloaded & installed the sounds if you want to use the tools.

SDA2Wav converts the audio files to .wav files, which are lossless in quality. Catalog Scraper downloads the MP3 files, which are lossy in quality.
In terms of quality and efficiency, you're better off using SDA2Wav.
